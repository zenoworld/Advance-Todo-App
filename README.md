# Advance-Todo-App
This is a to-do app with sophisticated features.
1. The LocalStorage will store your task.
2. By simply clicking on the 'SortByDate' option, you may sort your tasks according to the due dates of each work.
3. Simply click the 'SortByStatus' button to sort your task based on the task status. It indicates that the tasks at the bottom of the task list will be those that were marked as checked.
4. You may also search for your task by name in the Search Input section.
